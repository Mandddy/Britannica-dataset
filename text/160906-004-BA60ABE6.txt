Peter Martins,  (born October 27, 1946, Copenhagen, Denmark) Danish dancer and choreographer, known principally for his work with the New York City Ballet.
Martins began his dance training at the Royal Danish Ballet School in 1953, became a corps de ballet member in 1965, and was made a soloist two years later.
