Carmelo Anthony, byname Melo  (born May 29, 1984, Brooklyn, New York, U.S.) American professional basketball player who plays for the New York Knicks of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
Anthony, who grew up in a high-crime neighbourhood in Baltimore, Maryland, was sent by his mother to school in western Virginia for a better learning environment. After playing just one year of college basketball, Anthony left Syracuse to play professionally.
