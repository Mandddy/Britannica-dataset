Toni Collette, byname of Antonia Collette  (born November 1, 1972, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia) Australian actress known for her metamorphic performances in a wide range of roles.
Collette was raised in the Sydney suburb of Blacktown. At age 16 she accepted a scholarship from the Australian Theatre for Young People (1989), and she later briefly attended the National Institute of Dramatic Art. She dropped out to accept her first film role, in Spotswood (1992), opposite Anthony Hopkins and Russell Crowe.
