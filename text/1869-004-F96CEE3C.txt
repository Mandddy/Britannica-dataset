Kalanchoe (genus Kalanchoe), any of several species of succulent plants of the stonecrop family (Crassulaceae), popular for their easy culture indoors.
The most common species, valued for their unusual foliage, include the panda plant (Kalanchoe tomentosa); penwiper plant (K. marmorata); air plant, or maternity plant (K. pinnata); velvet leaf, or felt bush (K. beharensis); devilâ€™s backbone (K. daigremontiana); and South American air plant (K. fedtschenkoi).
