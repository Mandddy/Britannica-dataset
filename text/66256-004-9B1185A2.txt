Russell Banks,  (born March 28, 1940, Newton, Massachusetts, U.S.) American novelist known for his portrayals of the interior lives of characters at odds with economic and social forces.
Banks was educated at Colgate University (Hamilton, New York) and the University of North Carolina. From 1966 he was associated with Lillabulero Press, initially as editor and publisher. The press issued his first book of poems, Waiting to Freeze, in 1969.
