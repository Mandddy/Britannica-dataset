Laura Linney, in full Laura Leggett Linney  (born February 5, 1964, New York, New York, U.S.) American actress best known for playing strong yet vulnerable characters.
Linney was born into a theatrical family; her father was the playwright Romulus Linney. She graduated from Brown University in 1986 and later studied at the Arts Theatre School in Moscow and graduated from the Juilliard School (M.F.A.) in New York City in 1990. She immediately began performing on Broadway, eventually earning praise for her roles in Hedda Gabler and Six Degrees of Separation.
