Rachael Ray, in full Rachael Domenica Ray  (born August 25, 1968, Cape Cod, Massachusetts, U.S.) American chef and television personality, who promoted quick, easy-to-prepare meals through her television programs, lifestyle magazine, and extensive line of cookbooks.
Ray had experience in the kitchen from a young age, helping out in her family’s restaurants in Cape Cod. In her early 20s she pursued her culinary aspirations in New York City, where she started out behind Macy’s candy counter before managing its fresh foods department. In two years she became store manager and buyer for New York gourmet marketplace Agata &amp; Valentina before relocating to upstate New York. There she managed the pubs and restaurants of Sagamore Resort on Lake George.
