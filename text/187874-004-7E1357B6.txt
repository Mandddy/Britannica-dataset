Louise Erdrich, in full Karen Louise Erdrich  (born June 7, 1954, Little Falls, Minnesota, U.S.) American author whose principal subject is the Ojibwa Indians in the northern Midwest.
Erdrich grew up in Wahpeton, North Dakota, where her German American father and half-Ojibwa mother taught at a Bureau of Indian Affairs boarding school. She attended Dartmouth College (B.A., 1976) and Johns Hopkins University (M.A., 1979).
